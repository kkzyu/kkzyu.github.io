## About Me

I am a senior undergraduate at the School of Management, **Zhejiang University**, majoring in **Information Management & Information Systems**.

My research journey began under the supervision of Prof. [Siwei Fu](https://person.zju.edu.cn/siwei), with a focus on **AI-generated text detection**. In the summer of 2025, I worked as a research intern at the [State Key Lab of CAD&CG](http://www.cad.zju.edu.cn/zhongwen.html), collaborating with the renowned [ZJU IDG](https://zjuidg.org/) and [ZJU VAI](https://zjuvag.org/) groups. There, I contributed to the literature review and visualization design for a CHI submission regarding social media analysis.

Currently, I am privileged to lead a research project under the guidance of **CAS Academician**, Prof. [Yongbin Ruan](http://www.iasm.zju.edu.cn/2021/0525/c24255a2380362/page.htm). Additionally, I led the design of a university-level SRTP project investigating how AIGC-generated advertising content influences consumer decision-making.
